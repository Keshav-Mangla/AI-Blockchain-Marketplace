# AI Blockchain Marketplace

## Vision

Build a modern AI-powered decentralized marketplace that provides secure, transparent, and intelligent buying and selling experiences using Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain technology.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional marketplaces have several limitations:

- High platform fees
- Fake products
- Fake reviews
- Payment fraud
- Lack of transparency
- Centralized control
- Poor product recommendations

---

## Solution

Our platform will solve these problems using:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Blockchain
- Smart Contracts
- AI Fraud Detection
- AI Recommendation System
- AI Search
- Secure Payments
- Seller Verification

---

## Target Users

- Buyers
- Sellers
- Businesses
- Startups
- Digital Product Creators

---

## Business Model

- Premium Seller Membership
- AI Credits
